Cars

In a world where cars live and breathe like humans, racing is the heart and soul of their existence. Meet a young, brash racer headed for the biggest competition of his life. But when a detour strands him in a forgotten town, he discovers the warmth of friendship and the beauty of slowing down. Through unexpected connections and shared experiences, he learns valuable lessons about life and love, reigniting his passion for racing with a newfound humility and appreciation for the journey.

The Lion King

In the majestic African savannah, lions reign as kings, and Simba is born to inherit the throne. But his uncle's betrayal forces him into exile, where he grows up under the carefree tutelage of a meerkat and a warthog. As he matures, he reconnects with his childhood sweetheart and is called back to confront his past and reclaim his rightful place as king. Along the way, he discovers the true meaning of courage, friendship, and responsibility, embracing his destiny with a lion's heart and a human spirit.

One Hundred and One Dalmatians

Told from the perspective of Pongo, a dalmatian with a deep bond to his human owner, Roger, the story unfolds in a world where pets are cherished members of the family. When Pongo falls for Perdita, owned by Anita, their love brings their humans closer together, forming a tight-knit family unit. But their happiness is shattered when their litter of puppies is stolen by the infamous Cruella De Vil, who sees them as nothing more than fashion accessories. With the help of a motley crew of animal allies and compassionate humans, they embark on a daring rescue mission to save their pups and thwart Cruella's wicked plans, proving that love knows no bounds, whether you have two legs or four.
